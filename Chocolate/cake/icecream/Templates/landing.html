About Us

Reality is a cutting-edge freelancing platform designed to simplify the process of job hunting and career development. Our platform connects skilled professionals with a wide range of employers and businesses in need of talent, offering a seamless user experience. With a strong focus on accessibility, ease of use, and efficiency, Reality enables freelancers to browse job listings, apply for opportunities, and secure contracts with just a click of a button. Whether you're looking for part-time gigs, full-time remote work, or project-based assignments, Reality is the ultimate solution to kickstart your freelancing journey.

Reality was founded by Oluwadamilola Oyeyipo, a visionary leader and tech entrepreneur who serves as the CEO of the company. Oluwadamilola’s passion for technology and entrepreneurship is rooted in his belief that freelancing should be simple, stress-free, and rewarding for everyone. With a background in software engineering and hands-on experience in web development, he envisioned Reality as a platform where professionals can thrive in a flexible work environment. Under his leadership, Reality has grown to become a trusted name in the freelancing world, providing opportunities for thousands of freelancers worldwide to achieve their career goals.

Founded in August 2024, Reality was established to bridge the gap between freelancers and companies, ensuring both sides have access to the tools they need to succeed. At Reality, we understand the challenges freelancers face when searching for reliable work and navigating complex platforms. That’s why we designed Reality to offer stress-free job applications, transparent communication with potential employers, and the freedom to work on your own terms. Our mission is to make freelancing not just a career option, but an empowering and flexible lifestyle.

Our core values at Reality center around innovation, simplicity, and user empowerment. We aim to continuously evolve our platform to meet the ever-changing demands of the freelance workforce while providing a user-friendly interface that prioritizes functionality and user satisfaction. We are committed to creating a supportive ecosystem where freelancers can build their portfolios, develop meaningful client relationships, and enjoy a stress-free career path. As we move forward, Reality remains dedicated to making freelancing as easy and accessible as possible for all.
